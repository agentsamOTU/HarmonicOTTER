On the player's turn they can perform two actions

This includes playing cards (bottom right), using the accelerate or brake pedals (bottom left), or a combination of both

The goal is to get your gear level(bottom left in red) to match the objective in the top right

Once the player has selected their actions they can hit end turn to move on

As the player wins turns the bar at the top will fill up

Eventually the race will finish and the player will return to the menu

Try to complete all 3 levels

Completed levels cannot be played again

To access the debug menu, click the option button on the menu 10 times
